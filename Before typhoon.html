Before Typhoon

Don'ts

Don't get carried away by rumors/ do not spread rumors. Only rely on verified iformation from legitimate sources. 

Never assume and don't underestimate the extent of a disaster.

Don't forget to check the news.

Don't think that it won't happen.

Don't waste food or other necessities

Before Typhoon

Do's

Board up windows or put some storm shutters.

Ready your emergency kit.

Secure important documents.

Monitor the news about weather disturbances.

Have food that do not require cooking.

Evacuate if needed.
