Things you need in a survival kit
Water
-In case of water shortage
Canned Goods
-after a typhoon, natural resources of foods might run out
Flashlight
-when typhoon occurs, power shortage is expected to happen
Battery-powered radio
-since power shortage might occur, a battery-powered radio will help you keep updated with the current events without the use of television
Extra batteries
-if the absence of electrical power goes longer, use these extra batteries to keep your flaslighht and radio alive and working. 
First aid kit
-injuries after typhoon occurence is also expected

Multi-purpose tool
-during/after a typhoon, different tools will be harder to keep. having a multi-purpose will definitely be handy
Copies of personal documents
-your personal documents might go missing or get wet when a typhoon comes. It is important to have backup
Extra cash
-because of power shortage and destroyedbuildings, it will be harder to withdraw from the bank
Whistle
-in case you get lost or get stuck in an emergency situation, you can use the whistle to inform and alert people around you
Matches
-at times of typhoon, gasoline can get  pretty expensive. You can use some matches to save some cash to cook or light up yourlight sources
Extra clothes
-as a result of a typhoon, your clothes might get wet or go missing
