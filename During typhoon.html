During Typhoon

Don'ts

Do not cross on flooded areas or rivers

Do not use wet of washed out electric appliance.

Do not panic

Don't forget to turn off the main sources of electricity if you see signs of water rising.

During Typhoon

Do's

Keep calm.

Monitor the weather news and keep updated.

Avoid flood and landslide prone areas.

Reserve extra foods, water and other necessities.

Make sure that important things like personal documents and other stuff are safe from water floods and rains.

Keep the phone lines open to facilitate rescue and communication in times of emergencies.
